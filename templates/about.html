About DUT Safety & Maintenance

Welcome to the new DUT Safety & Maintenance website. This platform is designed to provide students and staff members with a quick and easy way to report issues related to safety and maintenance on campus. Whether you notice a broken handrail, a burnt-out light, or any other potential hazard, simply log in to the website and submit a report. Our team will receive your report and attend to the issue as soon as possible.
